Homura Akemi

Homura Akemi is a time-traveling magical girl and the main character of Puella Magi Madoka Magica. Her magical powers revolve around manipulating time, allowing her to freeze and rewind time for short periods. She initially appears as a cold and distant person, but her true motivations are gradually revealed throughout the series.
